||PABLO DÍAZ||

Musician, Sound Artist, and Artisan.

I have a Master's degree in Science and Art of Glass and Ceramics from the NOVA School of Science and Technology and a Master's degree in Sound Art from the Universitat de Barcelona.

My work focuses on the creation of dialogues between craft, material and technology. I have professional training and experience in the fields of music, art and crafts, ranging from education to research and interdisciplinary applications.
