| CLAI |


The work explores technology as a sensitive projection of intelligence. Different kinds of intelligence and knowledge are revealed, simulated and concealed by the participants and the artificial intelligence. This process materializes in an audiovisual document in which every step is recorded. The stages of this game consist of actions such as editing and simulating the editing of content related to the creation of a handmade piece (clay plaque).
