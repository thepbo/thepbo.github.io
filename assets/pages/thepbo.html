THE_P.B.O

|Glass|

The_PBO explores glass and its behavior in relation to sound. The nature of glass, as well as the objects that emerge from its interaction with humans, is full of details. These details can be perceived directly or subtly as sound and sonic features.

The_PBO interacts with glass and its crafts, such as glass blowing, lampworking and glass fusing, experiencing sound phenomena on a scale that can lead to a technical approach, a performative action or a register that is understood as witnessing a particular phenomenon. The output usually refers to hybrid catalogs, diaries and performative research such as live actions, ASMR moments (online or not) and workshops.
