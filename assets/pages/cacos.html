Colectivo C.A.C.O.S

| Vidro-Dramas Iluminadas |

The project consists of choreographed action episodes supported by sound, whose script is based on the properties of glass. These actions aim at creation of landscapes and a possible collection and interplay of interactions between glass entities, thus giving the material a double poetic nature: "the substrate that transitions between entity and background", resulting in 4 episodes in which the juxtaposition of different planes and movements brings to life the script and the world in which it is set..

The visual part of the project is inspired by the work of the designer Lei Takeuchi (Japan), and the audiovisual proposal is inspired by the Balinese theater, resulting in a multidisciplinary spectacle. The sound of the proposal comes from the same material, from its destruction and interaction with various devices (glass cutters, sticks, hands, ceramics, and others). The sound can be prepared as an audiovisual element or live, depending on the conditions of the room and its technical possibilities. The aim of the sound part is to create a coherent basis for the actions that take place both on the dramatic and visual levels.
