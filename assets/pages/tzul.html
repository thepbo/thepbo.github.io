T.Z.U.L

|CERAMICS|

T.Z.U.L explores ceramics and their behavior in relation to sound. The nature of ceramic materials, as well as the objects that emerge from their interaction with humans, is full of details. These details can be perceived directly or subtly as sound and sonic features.

T.Z.U.L interacts with ceramics and their different stages after sintering and experiences sound phenomena to an extent that can lead to a technical approach, a performative action or a register that is understood as witnessing a particular phenomenon. The output usually refers to hybrid catalogs, diaries and performative research such as live actions, ASMR moments (online or not) and workshops.
